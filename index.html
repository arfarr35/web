AARON FARR

Writer, Designer, Creator, Filmmaker

A24

In 2019, I spent five months interning for one of my favorite companies in entertainment, A24. From premiering films to celebrities to overseeing digital distribution sales, I truly got the most out of this position. Perhaps the most important skill I learned at A24 was collaboration. Working together with passionate, hardworking people made this one of the best experiences I have ever had in my career.

PROBIE

Early 2018, I began developing a short film about firefighters, with Dakota Taylor. We spent hours brainstorming the story and considering who would be on the crew for this production. The plan was to enter the short into Campus Movie Fest. As a producer, I spent time developing the story, finding the best student cast and crew possible, and also ended up being an extra in the film. We made this short film with only a week allotted for production and post-production. With some hard work, late nights, and a really long XLR cable we made this film happen.

Probie won Best Director, Top Jury Award, will be competing in Terminus this June,
and will be screened at the 2019 Cannes Film Festival.

PIGASUS PICTURES

From October to November, I was offered a position as a production assistant on the set of Pigasus Pictures newest film, Ms. White Light. This was an incredible experience I never would've dreamed to get in Southern Indiana. Many long days and nights were spent helping this production. My time as a PA allowed me to observe how a feature film is organized and managed from start to finish.

Here's the Scoop

Our team decided to take a fun, informational route. My tasks were to produce graphics, direct, and edit this short informational.
