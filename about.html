WHAT I DO

A LITTLE INSIGHT ABOUT ME.

MOVING PICTURES

For as long as I've lived I have loved movies. From moving pictures and sound, movies build a new world inside of our own creating an escape to another reality. They can fill us with any emotion imaginable and even some that are unattainable otherwise. They inspire us to create and make change. The ability to provoke emotions through music videos, advertisements, television shows, and films is astounding and something I wish to attain as I continue producing content.

No matter what I end up pursuing as a career, it will be centered around filmmaking. To create art as others have and to inspire emotion through visuals is a dream of mine.

THE PURSUIT

I am currently studying media with a focus in cinema at Indiana University. As I move through my courses, I grow more and more excited to blaze my trail in the film industry.
